Brilent Bootcamp

Brilent Bootcamp is a 6-week data analytics training program where you will get the opportunity to network and be mentored by seasoned data science professionals on real-world projects.

At the end of the program, you will be able to present your hard work in front of top employers and be ready to land your dream job!

What makes us unique

Full-time and part-time mentors who are at the top of their fields. Work alongside former and current data scientists from Facebook, LinkedIn, Google, Twitter, and other top tier companies.

Real-world projects. Build your portfolio and solve real-world problems (some are from companies that have an exclusive partnership with Brilent). Here are just a few awesome things our fellows have built: link1, link2, and link3.

Real industry skills. We help you develop problem-solving, critical thinking, and business presentation skills, which will turn you into a more qualified and marketable job applicant.

Exclusive resources. Have unlimited access to our online resources.

Free tuition. The program is free for admitted fellows – no hidden cost! Employers pay a tuition fee when they successfully hire our fellows.

No PhD required. We have worked with plenty of superstar data scientists who don’t have a PhD. We encourage people with all educational levels and quantitative background to apply!

Online Academy

Can’t commit 6 weeks of your time? Not ready for the bootcamp? No problem.
You may want to join Brilent’s Online Academy, which offers the expertise and professional network you need to succeed in Data Science and Analytics.

Membership includes

Unlimited access. Learn what you want, when you want, from our entire online course library.

Rich content. Courses cover core technical skills (SQL, R, statistical methods, and so on) and real data science case studies.

Seasoned mentors. Watch and learn from the experts of leading companies like Facebook, Google, LinkedIn, Twitter, eBay, Coupons.com and Shutterfly, who are willing to share their firsthand experience in Big Data and Analytics, and provide valuable career advice.

Online community. Have access to exclusive job/internship opportunities, and continuous advice from mentors to ensure long-term career success.

Who should enroll

We welcome all students (undergrads, masters, and PhDs) and postdocs with a quantitative background (in Physics, Statistics, Math, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering, Operations Research, Business, Economics, Management Sciences, etc.), who are looking to enter the exciting world of Data Science and Analytics.

Professionals seeking to change or advance their careers: those early in their career or experienced career professionals who are looking to switch to Data Science and Analytics.
